Top 10 Tourist Places in India

A simple beginner-friendly HTML page about famous travel destinations.

1. Taj Mahal

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

2. Jaipur

Location: Rajasthan

Jaipur, the Pink City, is famous for its forts, palaces, and vibrant markets, including Amer Fort and City Palace.

3. Goa

Location: Western India

Goa is known for its sandy beaches, Portuguese heritage, seafood, and lively culture.

4. Kerala Backwaters

Location: Kerala

The Kerala backwaters are a network of serene canals and lakes, best explored by houseboat.

5. Varanasi

Location: Uttar Pradesh

Varanasi is one of the oldest living cities in the world, known for its ghats, temples, and spiritual atmosphere.

6. Ladakh

Location: Jammu and Kashmir

Ladakh offers high-altitude deserts, Buddhist monasteries, and dramatic mountain landscapes.

7. Hampi

Location: Karnataka

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage site with ancient ruins, temples, and scenic boulder-strewn landscapes.

8. Udaipur

Location: Rajasthan

Udaipur is famous for its lakes, palaces, and romantic ambiance, including the Lake Palace and City Palace.

9. Darjeeling

Location: West Bengal

Darjeeling is a hill station known for tea gardens, scenic views of the Himalayas, and the historic mountain railway.

10. Mysore

Location: Karnataka

Mysore is known for the opulent Mysore Palace, vibrant festivals, and rich cultural heritage.
